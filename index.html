About Us

Taste Nepal. Feel Nature.

Simle Khaja Ghar combines authentic Nepalese cuisine with unforgettable nature experiences. Nestled in Dharan-20, Tamakham, we welcome travelers, hikers, food lovers, and families to enjoy warm hospitality inspired by Nepalese culture.

From flavorful khana sets and sizzling pork BBQ to peaceful hillside hiking trails, every experience is designed to refresh your soul and connect you with nature.
